How settlements work

Order in. Commission out. Payout settled.

Every order runs the same auditable path. Here's a single vendor order, worked end to end at an 18% commission rate.

1Order

Order captured

A guest pays at the stall. The order lands live in the dashboard with its vendor, currency and tax.

$24.00
Gross order value · incl. tax
2Commission

Commission applied

The configured rate for this vendor is netted off automatically — no spreadsheets, no manual maths.

− $4.32
18% commission · this vendor
3Payout

Payout settled

The vendor's net payout is calculated and queued for settlement, with a full audit entry attached.

$19.68
Net vendor payout · ready to settle

Commission rates are configurable per vendor, per event or per category — the settlement engine applies the right one every time, and writes every step to the audit log.
