How it drafts

Four steps between your metrics and your LPs' inboxes.

Every month follows the same short path. You review and approve — the draft is already waiting.

01

Pull metrics

Live numbers flow in from your stack — revenue, growth, runway and the rest — so the update starts with real data.

02

Draft the update

A full monthly update writes itself from those numbers — highlights, context and the story behind each change.

03

Tune the tone

Pick Concise, Detailed, Punchy or Vulnerable, and toggle which sections matter this month. The draft rewrites instantly.

04

Send & track

Deliver to your LP list in one click, then watch opens and replies land in your send history.
