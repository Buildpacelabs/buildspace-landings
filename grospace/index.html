From PDF to portfolio

Three steps from a signed PDF to a working portfolio.

No re-keying, no spreadsheets. Extraction, a human confirm, then everything downstream builds itself.

Extract

Read the lease

Drop in a lease — clean text or a scanned copy. Gemini 2.5 Pro reads it in text or vision mode and returns 60+ structured fields, each cited back to the source clause.

Confirm & Activate

Confirm the fields

Review the extraction with confidence scores beside every value. One click to activate — and the platform generates the records that used to take a week.

  • Payment records generated

Lease extraction

Deal pipeline

A pipeline that moves with the deal.

A six-stage board tracks every deal from Sourcing through Closed. Drag a card to advance a stage; SQFT, rent, term and NOI travel with it so the picture stays honest.

  • Drag-and-drop Kanban across Sourcing, LOI, Diligence and Closed
  • Deal economics on every card, filtered by asset type
  • Copilot flags deals that overrun their close date
