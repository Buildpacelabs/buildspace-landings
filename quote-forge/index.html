Where margin escapes

Every deal is a negotiation. Margin leaks in the gaps.

By the time a discounted quote reaches finance, the margin is already gone. QuoteForge catches it at the moment the rep builds the quote — not in a month-end review.

Rogue discounting High

Reps discount to hit quota, then finance discovers the deal landed below the margin floor.

Slow approvals Medium

Approval chains live in email and Slack, so hot deals stall for days waiting on a sign-off.

Inconsistent proposals Medium

Every rep rebuilds the proposal in a deck, so pricing, terms and branding drift deal to deal.

Margin blind spots Critical

No one sees the true margin of a quote until it's booked — the single sharpest driver of leakage.

Configure · price · close

From catalog to signed proposal, in one flow.

QuoteForge turns a product catalog and a set of pricing rules into a quote a rep can build in minutes and a desk can approve with confidence.

STEP 01

Configure

Reps assemble the deal from your catalog — seats, modules, support tiers and services — with dependencies and bundles enforced automatically.

STEP 02

Price & guard

Live pricing shows the margin as it's built. AI guidance recommends how far to discount — and blocks anything that breaks the floor.

STEP 03

Approve & propose

Out-of-band deals route to the right approver instantly, and a branded proposal generates the moment the quote clears.
